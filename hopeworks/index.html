Ranger Tugs R-43 S/CB features and specifications sheet

An undertaking described during onboarding at Fluid Motion, I was tasked with redesigning the printed feature and specification sheets for around 20 different boats across two brands. First up was the R-43 Sedan/Command Bridge, the flagship of the Ranger Tugs line. Here, I provided a larger sense of flow between pages (the first and last are printed on the same side of a tabloid sheet, ditto for the second and third images), a stronger form of uniformity and organization, and a unique visual identity for the brand. Only the spec sheet for the R-43 is currently published, however, the other Ranger Tugs and all Cutwater Boats spec sheets are in their final draft stages.

Angel Of The Winds Casino Resort table games informational/how to play touchscreen

Asked to design new informational touchscreens for most venues at the casino, the table games touchscreen proved to be the most intense. Between creating a unique style that matched elements of the casino as a whole and the table games specifically, accurately (and legally) applying the game rules to each page, and linking the screens correctly between them all, it proved to be a complex assignment.

Lunar New Year Concert - 2020

At the end of 2019, Angel Of The Winds was the last casino in the area to book and advertise a musical guest for the Lunar Near Year (in January 2020). Once booked, I was tasked with quickly coming up with a unique look that stood out from the other casino's campaigns - dominated by red and gold coloring - that had already been live for at least a month. Right out of the gate, I drew inspiration from East Asian watercolor paintings and plums - both in the trees and literal color.

Xpansion Grand Opening passport

After several delays, the "Xpansion" at Angel Of The Winds was formally unveiled in late summer 2019. For the opening, the experience was gamified to engage patrons to visit all the new venues and collect potential rewards from raffles. I was tasked with creating the "passport" brochure, with stamping areas and map.

Collectable Game Day buttons - 2018

An annual tradtion for EWU Eagle Store design interns, I was tasked with creating unique buttons for the Fall 2018 football season, in addition to additional collateral to advertise the promotion.

The Rocky Planets - motion visualization

Yep, that is my own narration! In my motion design class at Eastern Washington University, we were taked with creating a educational visualization of at least a minute in length. Beyond its slick visual look, I took extra attention to minor details - such as accurate planet sizes in transitions - and added a dollop of humor in as well (subjective).

David Lang is a graphic designer at Angel Of The Winds Casino Resort. There, he designs advertising for various campaigns for public and employee consumption, and oversees the casino’s website and all TVs on property. He is a VCD graduate from Eastern Washington University, and formerly worked at the EWU Eagle Store as their in-house graphics assistant. David formerly worked at EWU’s design computer lab for over three years, and interned at Spokane Regional Transportation Council, creating web maps and promotional material.

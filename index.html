David Lang is a graphic designer in Everett, Washington. He previously resided at HopeWorks Social Enterprises, designing creative collateral and overseeing multiple marketing channels. David previously worked at Fluid Motion LLC, creating marketing material and advertising for multiple boat makes, and Angel Of The Winds Casino Resort, designing advertising for promotions and events. He is a VCD graduate from Eastern Washington University, having also worked at the EWU Eagle Store, the EWU design lab, and SRTC.

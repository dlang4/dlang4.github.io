David Lang is a graphic designer in Everett, WA. He formerly worked at Angel Of The Winds Casino Resort, where he designed advertising for various campaigns, and ran the casino’s website and all TVs on property. He is a VCD graduate from Eastern Washington University, and formerly worked at the EWU Eagle Store as their in-house graphics assistant. David also formerly worked at EWU’s design computer lab for over three years, and interned at Spokane Regional Transportation Council.

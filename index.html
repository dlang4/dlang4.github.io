Bio

Hello! I am a graduate from attending Eastern Washington University specializing in geographic information systems and visual communication design. I currently work as a GIS intern at Spokane Regional Transportation Council, regularly updating their road construction map in addition to other projects, including assisting in updating the agency's Metropolitan Transportation Plan. I formerly worked a lab consultant in EWU's designated multimedia computer lab.

This website

Originally created for my web design classes, this site provides a general professional overview of myself. The homepage runs entirely on HTML & CSS animations, and the other pages have light instances of jQuery & Javascript. In addition, the homepage also utilizes responsive design and has three distinct layouts depending on screen size; desktop, tablet, and phone. The color scheme was approximated from a style tile of my own personality and interests.
